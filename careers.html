How to Apply

For maximum consideration, please submit the following three completed documents to workforceconnections@workforceconnections.org as soon as possible. If you would like to be considered for more than one position, please outline your interest in your cover letter.

  1. Resume
  2. Cover Letter, and
  3. Workforce Connections, Inc. Application (PDF)

Incomplete application materials will not be considered.

To complete the application, download the latest version of Adobe Reader.

  1. Save application to your computer.
  2. Complete the application.
  3. Attach the application to an email with your other documents.
  4. Email to: workforceconnections@workforceconnections.org

NOTE:  We strongly encourage you to submit your application electronically using the above mentioned process; however, if you are having technical difficulties due to your current version of Adobe, it is permissible to mail a hard copy or drop it off in person.

Please do not fax application materials.

If you encounter any problems attaching or sending the application, please call 800-742-5627 or 608-789-5601 for assistance.
