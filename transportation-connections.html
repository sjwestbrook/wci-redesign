Transportation Connections

What is Transportation Connections?

Transportation Connections is a program that assists eligible employed individuals with a vehicle repair or purchase.

To be eligible for the program, you must:

  1. Be employed (not self-employed),
  2. Meet income guidelines,
  3. Be able to repay your debt,
  4. Submit all required documentation, including proof of insurance, and
  5. Pay a non-refundable application fee of $50.

Call 608-789-5627 for more information. Please note, new funds will not be available until Spring 2016!

Requirements

Requirements

If you meet eligibility requirements and are approved for a loan, you will:

  1. Receive a 0% interest loan for up to $1000 for a car repair or up to $5000 for a car purchase.
  2. Pay an administrative fee upfront ($200 for purchase loan; $100 for repair loan).
  3. Be required to pay all tax, title, and license fees (if purchase).
  4. Be required to participate in Financial Literacy with On-time Devices.
  5. Be required to submit your vehicle title (for repair loans only) to Workforce Connections, Inc. to hold until loan is repaid.
  6. Make monthly payments of $150 for vehicle purchase loans or $100 for vehicle repair loans.
