Windows to Work

What is Windows to Work?

Windows to Work promotes self-sufficiency for individuals returning to the community through skill development and cognitive behavioral training. Services are available within the following correctional facilities in western Wisconsin:

  • Jackson Correctional Institutions (JCI),
  • New Lisbon Correctional Institutions (NLCI), and
  • Prairie Du Chien Correctional Institutions (PDCI).

For eligibility and to apply:

Participants must be incarcerated at a participating institution and be within 12 months of release. For more information inform your institution social worker or contact Sandy Turner at 1-608-790-8137.
